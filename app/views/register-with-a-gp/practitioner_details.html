Nurse Practitioners

Nurse Practitioners are registered nurses who have received three years additional university education. They are qualified to diagnose medical problems, prescribe medications and make referrals to other healthcare professionals.

In a GP practice, a nurse practitioner typically deals with many types of illness, such as:
  • Sore throats and ongoing colds and coughs
  • Chest infections
  • Allergic reactions
  • Childhood illness such as Mumps, Chicken Pox, Measles
  • ... many other minor illnesses
